# Privacy Policy for TubeAI Studio

**Last Updated: March 27, 2026**

TubeAI Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1.1 Personal Data
When you use TubeAI Studio, we may collect certain personally identifiable information, including:
- **Google Account Information**: If you sign in using Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to personalize your experience.
- **User-Generated Content**: Scripts, thumbnails, and video ideas generated within the app.

### 1.2 Device Information
We may collect information about your mobile device, including:
- Device model and manufacturer.
- Operating system version.
- Unique device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's features.

- Improve our AI models and app performance.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Cloud Storage**: Your data may be stored using Google Firebase or other secure cloud services.

- **Google Gemini API**: For content generation and analysis.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## 5. Your Rights
